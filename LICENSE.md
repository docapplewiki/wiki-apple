## 🛡️ Tips for Safer Use

Using any Roblox executor violates Roblox's Terms of Service. Minimize your risk with these best practices:

1. **Use a dedicated alt account** — Never run scripts on your main Roblox account. Always create a separate account exclusively for executor use.
2. **Prefer private servers** — Scripting in uncrowded or private servers significantly reduces the chance of being reported by other players.
3. **Avoid aggressive scripts in competitive games** — Scripts that provide blatant advantages (god mode, kill-all) in anti-cheat-protected games carry the highest ban risk.
4. **Only download from official sources** — Always get the **appleware IPA file download** or macOS build from the [official apple ware executor website](https://docapplewiki.github.io/wiki-apple/) or this GitHub repository. Third-party mirrors are unvetted and may contain malware.
5. **Monitor the appleware discord server** — The development team posts immediate alerts in Discord when Roblox pushes a detection patch. Enable notifications for `#announcements` so you always know when to pause executor use.
6. **Keep Appleware updated** — The **appleware executor update** system is automatic, but always verify you're on the latest version shown in the `#releases` channel. Outdated versions are more detectable.
7. **On iOS: re-sign before the 7-day expiry** — If using AltStore (not TrollStore), your IPA certificate expires every 7 days. Set a calendar reminder to re-sign before it lapses, or switch to TrollStore for a permanent install.
8. **Don't modify or redistribute the IPA** — Distributing modified builds of the **appleware executor IPA** violates the project's license and puts other users at risk.

## 🔧 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ "Appleware won't open — 'unidentified developer' error on macOS"
**Fix:** This is macOS Gatekeeper blocking an app from outside the App Store — it's not a virus warning. Go to *System Settings* → *Privacy & Security* → scroll down and click **"Open Anyway"** next to the Appleware entry. You only need to do this once.

---

### ❌ "IPA won't install / shows error in AltStore"
**Fix:** Ensure AltStore is up to date and your Apple ID is signed in. If the error persists, try refreshing your AltStore credentials and reinstalling. Also confirm you downloaded the **appleware IPA** from the [official source](https://docapplewiki.github.io/wiki-apple/) — corrupted or modified IPA files from third-party sites frequently fail to install.

---

### ❌ "Appleware crashes immediately on launch (macOS)"
**Fix:** Verify your macOS version meets the minimum requirement (macOS 13+). If you're on an Intel Mac, ensure Rosetta 2 is installed (`softwareupdate --install-rosetta` in Terminal). For M-series Macs, try deleting and reinstalling the app fresh from the [official page](https://docapplewiki.github.io/wiki-apple/).

---

### ❌ "Scripts execute but nothing happens in Roblox"
**Fix:** The script may be outdated or game-patched. Check the **appleware discord server** `#script-hub` channel for updated versions. Also make sure Appleware has fully attached to Roblox before executing — wait for the green "Attached" indicator in the UI.

---

### ❌ "App expired on iOS (AltStore users)"
**Fix:** AltStore-sideloaded apps expire after 7 days. Open AltStore and tap **Refresh All** to re-sign Appleware for another 7 days. For a permanent solution, install via TrollStore if your iOS version is supported.

---

### ❌ "Appleware doesn't detect Roblox on macOS"
**Fix:** Ensure Roblox is fully loaded into a game world (not just the launcher or home screen) before launching Appleware. Also check that Appleware has been granted necessary permissions under *System Settings* → *Privacy & Security* → *Accessibility*.

---

### ❌ "I can't find the appleware exe download"
**Fix:** There is no **appleware exe**. Appleware is an Apple-only product — the macOS version is a `.app` bundle inside a `.dmg`, and the iOS version is an **IPA file**. If you're on Windows, Appleware is not the right tool for your platform — consider Atlantis or Solara instead.
